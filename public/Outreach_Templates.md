## Personalization Framework

For each outreach, spend 5-7 minutes personalizing:

1. **Research the person:** Check their LinkedIn, company website, recent news
2. **Find a connection:** Look for shared interests, experiences, or goals
3. **Reference something specific:** Mention a project, achievement, or article they've shared
4. **Show value first:** Explain why you're reaching out and what value you offer
5. **Make it easy to respond:** Ask a clear, specific question
6. **Include your best contact:** Email, WhatsApp, or LinkedIn

---

## Outreach Sequence

**Day 1:** Initial outreach (email or LinkedIn message)  
**Day 4:** Follow-up if no response  
**Day 8:** Second follow-up with new angle or value  
**Day 12:** Final follow-up before moving on  

---

## Response Rate Optimization

- **Personalization:** 5-7 minutes per message increases response by 40%
- **Timing:** Send Tuesday-Thursday, 9am-11am for best response
- **Subject Lines:** Use names and specific references (increases open rate by 30%)
- **Length:** Keep messages short (3-4 sentences for LinkedIn, 150-200 words for email)
- **Call to Action:** Make it easy (yes/no question, specific time)

## Tracking Outreach

Log in your Lead Tracker:

- Response received (yes/no/maybe)
- Next action
- Follow-up date

---

## Do's and Don'ts

**Do:**
- ✅ Personalize every message
- ✅ Research before reaching out
- ✅ Provide value first
- ✅ Keep it short and clear
- ✅ Follow up persistently
- ✅ Track everything
- ✅ Be authentic and genuine

**Don't:**
- ❌ Send generic mass messages
- ❌ Ask for something immediately
- ❌ Make it about you (make it about them)
- ❌ Send too many follow-ups (max 3)
- ❌ Give up after one rejection
- ❌ Forget to follow up
- ❌ Be pushy or aggressive
