## Tips for Success

1. **Consistency Over Perfection:** 2 hours every day beats 10 hours once a week
2. **Personalization:** Spend 5-7 minutes personalizing each application/message
3. **Track Everything:** Data drives optimization
4. **Follow Up:** Most opportunities come from persistence, not initial contact
5. **Quality Over Quantity:** 5 great applications beat 20 generic ones
6. **Share Your Journey:** LinkedIn content builds your personal brand
7. **Learn & Adjust:** Review metrics weekly and adjust your approach
8. **Celebrate Wins:** Acknowledge progress, no matter how small

---

## Troubleshooting

### Low Response Rate
- Personalize more (spend 10 minutes per application)
- Target better companies (research before applying)
- Improve portfolio (add case studies, projects)
- Update LinkedIn profile (add professional photo, headline)

### No Meetings Scheduled
- Follow up on applications after 1 week
- Increase outreach volume
- Improve message quality (focus on value, not ask)
- Add more LinkedIn content (shows expertise)

### Losing Motivation
- Review progress (you've done more than you think)
- Celebrate small wins
- Take 1 day off per week
- Connect with other job seekers (accountability)
